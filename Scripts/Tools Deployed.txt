Scripts & Tools Used:
---------------------

[01] CloakQuest3r
     Function: Aggressive enumeration and surface-level reconnaissance
     Access: https://github.com/spyboy-productions/CloakQuest3r

[02] SHODAN
     Function: Eyes on the open net. Exposed devices, forgotten ports.
     Access: https://www.shodan.io

[03] crt.sh
     Function: Certificate archives. Past and present fingerprints.
     Access: https://crt.sh

[04] DNS Dumpster
     Function: Map the skeleton. DNS relationships, host clusters.
     Access: https://dnsdumpster.com

[05] ICANN WHOIS
     Function: Domain identity, lineage, ownership traces.
     Access: https://lookup.icann.org

[06] WhatIsMyIP
     Function: Position verification. Know where the signal originates.
     Access: https://www.whatismyip.com
